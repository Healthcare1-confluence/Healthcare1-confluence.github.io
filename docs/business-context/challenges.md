# Industry Challenges

## Problem Statement: Client Context

A healthcare contact centre team handles thousands of daily calls across claims, benefits, and appointment management. Members call with specific questions but agents must navigate multiple disconnected systems:

- **Claims Portal** — Claim status and historical data
- **Benefits Document Repository** — Plan coverage details
- **EHR Scheduling System** — Provider availability and appointments

### Why This Process is Time-Consuming

1. **No unified AI layer** exists to classify intent and route to the correct backend
2. **Multi-turn conversations lose context** across HTTP requests
3. **Appointment scheduling requires** checking pre-authorization before booking
4. **HIPAA compliance** must be maintained across every interaction without exception

### Current State Limitations

Without AI automation, handling quality depends on:
- Individual agent knowledge
- Manual system navigation
- Inconsistent service quality
- High operational costs
- Compliance risk

This makes it difficult to consistently and quickly serve members at scale.
